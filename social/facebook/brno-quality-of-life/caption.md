# Five small C++29 papers you will actually use

## Body
Underneath the Brno safety headlines, WG21 cleared five small papers you will feel every day, all targeting C++29. P2287 lets designated initializers reach through a base class. P3091 adds map.get(), an optional<mapped_type&> that does not insert and works on const. P3668 lets you =default the postfix ++/-- operator. P3125 standardizes std::pointer_tag_pair and quietly leans on the reflection operator ^^ to count spare alignment bits. And P3248 makes intptr_t and uintptr_t mandatory instead of optional. Maintenance, not keynotes -- but every one removes a daily papercut.
