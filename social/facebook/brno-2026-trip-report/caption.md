# After C++26, WG21 turns to undefined behavior

## Body
C++26 is done. So the first WG21 meeting since -- Brno, June 8-13, 2026 -- set the tone for C++29, the safety release. Per Herb Sutter's trip report, the headline was P3596R3, two annexes cataloguing every case of undefined behavior in the standard, with P3100 scheduling a case-by-case program to close them. Profiles kept moving, and P3097R3 brought virtual-function contracts back after they were cut from the C++26 MVP. Reflection was not on the marquee -- which is exactly what shipping looks like. Three companion posts go deeper this week.
