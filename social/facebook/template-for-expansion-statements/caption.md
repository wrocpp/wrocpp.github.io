# template for: iterating reflections at compile time

## Body
Three primitives, one composition.

`^^T` reflects. `[: r :]` splices. `template for` unrolls a loop at compile time, instantiating the body once per iteration. Together they make the post-1 JSON serializer not a hack but the natural composition.

Part 4 of the wro.cpp reflection series. The last foundational primitive; everything from here builds on top.
