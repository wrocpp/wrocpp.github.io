# CppCon 2026 puts three language designers on one stage

## Body
CppCon 2026 is putting three language creators on one stage for the first time: Bjarne Stroustrup (C++), Guido van Rossum (Python), and Mads Torgersen (C#).

Three of the world's most-used languages, all facing the same questions -- safety, AI, and how to evolve without breaking everything -- from three different starting points. Should be a great conversation.

September 12-18, Aurora CO.
