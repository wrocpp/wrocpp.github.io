# The unit bug that crashed into Mars

## Body
In 1999 NASA lost a $327 million spacecraft because one program spoke pound-force seconds and another expected newton seconds. Silently wrong by 4.45x, for months, until the Mars Climate Orbiter broke apart in the atmosphere.

mp-units, the C++ library by Mateusz Pusz (Gdansk, ISO C++ committee), makes that bug a compile error. The unit is part of the type; mixing them either converts exactly or does not build. The demo runs live in your browser.

First post of a series that ends at P3045: the proposal to put units into C++29.

Read it: https://wrocpp.github.io/posts/unit-bug-mars-orbiter/
