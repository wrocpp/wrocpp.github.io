# C++ is writing down all of its undefined behavior

## Body
The biggest move at WG21 Brno was not a feature -- it was a catalogue. P3596R3 adds two annexes that enumerate every case of undefined behavior and every ill-formed-no-diagnostic-required corner in the standard, each with a stable, cross-linked name. P3100R6 turns that list into a program: the authors counted exactly 80 cases of core-language UB, and 56 of them (about 70 percent) are memory-safety bugs. The enforcement machinery -- erroneous behavior plus contracts -- already shipped in C++26. P3596 is the index, P3100 is the schedule, and the target is C++29.
