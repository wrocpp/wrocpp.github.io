# Meeting C++ 2026 lands in Berlin with a units-library keynote

## Body
Meeting C++, the main European C++ conference, returns to Berlin from 26 to 28 November 2026, hybrid so you can attend onsite or online. Two of the three keynotes are announced.

First is Mateusz Pusz, author of mp-units, the quantities-and-units library that drew unanimous support in its first committee review this spring and is heading toward C++29. We ran a six-part series on it, so the author giving a keynote is a fitting bookend. Second is Kate Gregory, always excellent on the human side of the craft. The third slot is open.

Worth a spot on the calendar: https://wrocpp.github.io/posts/meetingcpp-2026-pusz/
