# A new unit in one line

## Body
In 1958 MIT students measured the Harvard Bridge in the body lengths of Oliver Smoot: 364.4 smoots, where one smoot is 1.7018 m. Real code is full of units your domain invented, so extending a units library has to be cheap.

In mp-units a new unit is one declaration. A single line gives the name, the symbol, and the exact factor relative to the metre, and conversions, printing, and derived quantities work immediately. The new unit is a first-class citizen, not a special case.
