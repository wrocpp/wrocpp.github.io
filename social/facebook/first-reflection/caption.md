# Your first ^^: reflecting types and walking members

## Body
C++26 reflection in 12 lines.

`^^Point` reflects the type. `nonstatic_data_members_of` lists its fields. `identifier_of` gives you "x" and "y". The compiler does the work; you just walk the result.

This is part 2 of the wro.cpp reflection series, building the primitives that the part-1 teaser used.
