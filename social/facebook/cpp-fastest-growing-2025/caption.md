# C++ added more developers in 4 years than any other language

## Body
Herb Sutter's BeCPP Symposium 2026 keynote opened with a SlashData chart: C++ developer population grew 72% (9.5M -> 16.3M) over Q1 2022 to Q1 2025. Rust grew faster in percentage (137%) on a smaller base (5.1M total). Both stories true. C++ closed the gap on the top three languages, none passed, but added more developers in absolute terms than C and Rust combined.
