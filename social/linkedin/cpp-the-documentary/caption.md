# C++: The Documentary is now on YouTube

## Body
CultRepo's "C++: The Documentary" had its world premiere on May 28 at a special event in NYC's Financial District, followed by a panel discussion. The film is now available worldwide on YouTube.

The documentary traces C++ from its origins at Bell Labs in 1979 through the language's role in the infrastructure that runs the modern world. Sponsored by Hudson River Trading.

C++26 was finalized at Croydon in March 2026. GCC 16.1 shipped reflection five weeks later. The documentary arrives at exactly the moment when the language's history and its future are both in sharp focus.
