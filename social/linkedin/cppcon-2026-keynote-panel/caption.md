# CppCon 2026 puts three language designers on one stage

## Body
CppCon has never done a keynote panel. In 2026 it will.

Bjarne Stroustrup (C++), Guido van Rossum (Python), and Mads Torgersen (C#) on one stage -- three of the most-deployed languages in the world, represented by the people who shaped them. The timing is not an accident: every one of them is wrestling with the same pressures from a different starting point. Memory safety and the security spotlight. The role of AI in how code gets written. How a mature language keeps evolving without breaking the billions of lines already in production.

Hearing how the three designers make those trade-offs, and what they envy about each other's languages, is the kind of cross-pollination the C++ world rarely gets in one room.

CppCon 2026, September 12-18, Aurora CO.
